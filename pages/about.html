Our Story

From Living Room Messes to Soft Play Dreams

We started JungleJamboree in 2020 after constantly searching for safe, clean, and interactive play environments for our children. We designed our slides, ball pits, and obstacle zones from the ground up, combining active play with sensory development. Our mission is to make family playtime premium, easy, and unforgettable.
