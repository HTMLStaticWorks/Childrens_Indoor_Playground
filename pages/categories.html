Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about visiting JungleJamboree.

Do I need to sign a waiver?

Yes, all children must have a signed waiver from a parent or legal guardian before entering the play area. You can sign this online before your visit!

Are socks required?

Yes, for safety and hygiene, grip socks are required for all children playing on the equipment. Parents entering the play zone must also wear regular socks.

Can I bring outside food?

Outside food and drinks are not permitted, except for a cake if you have booked a private birthday party room. We have a cafe with many healthy snack options!

What is the age limit?

Our playground is designed for children ages 1 to 12. We have a dedicated Toddler Town area for children under 4 to play safely away from bigger kids.

Do adults have to pay?

No! Every child's admission includes up to two free adults. Additional adults are $5 each.

Do you clean the equipment?

Absolutely. We use non-toxic, hospital-grade sanitizers on all surfaces multiple times a day, and run a deep clean with UV sanitation every night.
